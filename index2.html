About Me

I am a recent graduate from the Rochester Institute of Technology with a B.S. in Game Design and Development and an immersion in Creative Writing. I am actively looking for career opportunities in the field of game/software development after having graduated December 2020.

I am proficient in C++, C#, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and in programs such as Maya, Unity, Unreal 4, and Visual Studio.

I've always been very passionate about video games, and I've eyed the game development program at RIT years before applying to it.
