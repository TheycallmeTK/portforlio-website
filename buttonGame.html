Features

An infinite clicker game where three types of circles are spawned in random locations on the screen. Click on the circles to increase your score and extend your time limit. Chain together clicks close together to combo and increase your gains

Three levels of circles (Red -> Blue -> Green) each level gives more time and score

Combo system

unique sound effects for each each circle when clicked (Royalty free sound clips)

Background music: Alan Walker - Fade
